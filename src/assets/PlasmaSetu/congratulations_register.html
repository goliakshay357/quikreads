Being a frontline worker, I was mentally prepared to be infected and so, the next day I lined up for my tests. Two days later, I was found positive and was immediately admitted to a hospital along with my wife who was infected because of me. The moral and social responsibility of saving someone’s life as a recovered COVID-19 patient is the need of the hour. That said, I too had fears and apprehensions of donating plasma but the will to help someone triumphed.

Pankaj Aundhiya

Plasma Donor
