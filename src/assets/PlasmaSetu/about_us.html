About Us

The Plasma Setu is a non-profit social project that aims to solve the problem of finding the Convalescent Plasma Donors for the Covid’19 patients in need of matching Plasma near them in India.

What we do

Our objective of helping the people find Plasma can be broken down in 3 processes:

  • Plasma Donor Registration: We get the willing donors to register on our platform as potential donors.
  • Covid Recovered Patients: We register the people who have suffered from Plasma in the past and encourage them to register as potential donors in future.
  • Looking for Plasma Donors: We register the requests of people who are in the need of Convalescent Plasma. We match these requests with our Donor data through intelligent analytics and help these people find matching donors nearby.

We also aim to educate people about Covid’19 and the health risks associated with it.
